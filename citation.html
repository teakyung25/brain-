How do the senses used in the lab relate to the parts of the brain?

  • Temporal lobe

    • The Temporal lobe is the part of the brain that controls the hearing, the facial recognition, emotion, and long term memory.

  • Cerebellum

    • The Cerebellum is the part of the brain that controls the Fine motor(muscle control), coordination, and balance.

  • Parietal lobe

    • The Parietal lobe is the part of the brain that controls the touch nerves, taste, and body awareness.

What does the Thalamus do?

It transfers sensory data to the sensory processing sensors. It sends stuff like sound to the sound processing centers.
